What is Recipe Route?

Recipe Route connects communities with their farms. The app generates recipes based on what you have at home, what’s coming out of the garden, or what’s available at nearby farms. If you’re a farmer, Recipe Route provides a place to share what you have for sale.

My hypothesis is simple: by creating recipes based on available ingredients, we can flip the usual patterns of supply and demand—drumming up demand based on supply. This could help farms grow and graze in ways that are geographically appropriate, aligned with their skills, and supported by their infrastructure.

For new farmers, I’m developing tools within Recipe Route to guide and coach you as you grow your farm. For households, the goal is to help you create healthy, easy, and beautiful meals from local ingredients.

In the end, I want Recipe Route to move local food from farm to table in the most economical, ecological, and enjoyable way possible.
